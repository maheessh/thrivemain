Welcome to Thrive Scholars SA2 Office!

The Thrive Scholars SA2 Office at the University of Miami is dedicated to supporting our scholars through various resources and services, ensuring a successful academic and personal journey. We're here to help you thrive!

Our Services

Mail Delivery

Secure and timely delivery of your packages and letters. Check your mail status quickly!

Snacks

Grab a quick bite or a refreshing drink during office hours. Perfect for study breaks!

Study Materials

Access essential study guides, textbooks, and other academic resources to help you succeed.

Check-In/Check-Out

Quick and easy process for checking into and out of office appointments or events.

Printing Services

High-quality printing available for your academic needs. Submit your print requests online.
